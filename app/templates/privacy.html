Privacy Policy for AI Email Sorter

Last Updated: July 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Email Sorter ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we request access to the following information:

  • Basic Profile Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by Google.
  • Gmail Access: We request permission to read and modify your emails (`gmail.readonly` and `gmail.modify scopes`). This is essential for the core functionality of the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To Provide Core Functionality: We access your emails to sort, summarize, and archive them based on the categories you define.
  • Data Processing: The content of your emails is sent to the Google Gemini API for the sole purpose of generating a summary and determining the appropriate category. The full body of your emails is temporarily stored in our database to allow you to view the original content but is not used for any other purpose.
  • User Account: Your email address is used as a unique identifier for your account to store your custom categories and processed email metadata.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store the following information in our secure database:

  • Your custom categories and their descriptions.
  • Metadata for processed emails, including the AI-generated summary, the assigned category, sender, and date.
  • The full HTML body of processed emails to allow you to view them within the app.

We do not store your Google password. Authentication is handled securely via OAuth 2.0. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.
